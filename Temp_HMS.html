Hospitals are essential part of our Modern life.People may suffer from different diseases or problems in our day to day problems. These may be the result of our day to day actions, or our habits, weather conditions, Influenza viruses. On an average thousands of people are visiting hospital every day. 3 times is the average of a person visiting to a hospital in a year. From this we can note that hospitals contains a large data that to be manged and keep a track of.

It is necessary for hospitals to keep track of all the data of patients, doctors, nurses, ward members and a lot of other hospital operations which may or may not be related to patients health. Keeping all this data on paper and storing into files is complex job to do and again we have to protect the data. Thus we are proposing a digital system that can store the data and update the data in real time with out having a tiring job to find the record. This would be helpful in many ways and this technically and economically feasible.
